What is a Deep Learning

Deep Learning: is a subset of machine learning that uses artificial neural networks with representation learning. It is based on the idea that machines can learn from data, identify patterns and make decisions with minimal human intervention. Deep learning algorithms use multiple layers to progressively extract higher-level features from the raw input. For example, in image processing, lower layers may identify edges, while higher layers may identify the concepts relevant to a human such as digits or letters or faces²³.
